NEXP POS Application

Fast, Secure & User-Friendly Point of Sale Solution for Retail & Restaurants

Smart Nest POS is a powerful and easy-to-use point of sale system designed to streamline sales, inventory, billing, and customer management for businesses of all sizes. Our POS solution works seamlessly for retail stores, supermarkets, restaurants, cafés, and service-based businesses.

The system supports real-time sales tracking, barcode scanning, GST/VAT-ready invoicing, multi-payment options, and detailed sales reports. With a clean interface and lightning-fast performance, your staff can serve customers quicker and more efficiently.

Smart Nest POS also includes inventory alerts, low-stock notifications, product variants, discounts, and user role management to give you full control over your business operations.
